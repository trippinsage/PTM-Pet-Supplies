Welcome to PTM Pet Supplies

Hello, pet lovers! We are your friendly, family-owned pet store in Marystown, NL, dedicated to helping your furry, feathery, or scaly friends thrive. From premium foods and safe, engaging toys to unique feeders, each item is carefully selected with love—because we consider your pets part of our family.

Stop by for our convenient Doggy U-Wash, browse our wide selection of quality products, and chat with our friendly team. We are proud to serve the Burin Peninsula with warm smiles!
